    Phase 3 VERIFICATION (`.planning/phases/03-publish-pipeline/03-VERIFICATION.md`) found that the

    network drop, the retry restarts from byte 0 instead of resuming from the last-acknowledged
    chunk.

    similar but not spec-compliant. The deviation is the ONLY outstanding gap in the v1 codebase.

    Phase 4 (Convenience & QA Polish) does not naturally cover this work — implementing real
    resumable upload requires dropping the googleapis SDK and replacing it with raw HTTP POST to
    `/upload/youtube/v3/videos?uploadType=resumable` + session URI tracking + Range PUTs. That is
    a substantial refactor (replaces the entire src/publish/uploader.ts + most of uploader.test.ts)
    that exceeds the "convenience & QA polish" framing of this phase.

    The verifier explicitly framed this as an operator decision and listed three resolution paths.
