<objective>
Replace the Plan 01-01 `all` CLI stub with a real driver that chains `prepare → render → publish` for `golazo all <folder>`.

The driver:
1. Calls `runPrepare(opts)`, emits the same frozen stdout line the standalone `prepare` handler emits.
2. On success, calls `runRender(opts)` and emits its frozen stdout line.
3. On success, calls `runPublish(opts)` and emits its frozen stdout line.
4. On any sub-stage failure, short-circuits, writes the original error to stderr, follows it with `golazo all: stage '<stage>' failed`, and exits 1 — leaving any successful earlier stage's side-effects (manifest, episode, thumb) on disk (no cleanup).
5. Forwards `--force` and `--channels-config` to all three sub-stages.

Purpose: Closes CLI-02 — the single chain-runner the operator uses for the 5+ games/week workflow. Eliminates three separate CLI invocations per game.

Output: `golazo all <folder>` works against the committed `tests/fixtures/golazo/leo/2026-05-13_vs_united_3-1/` fixture with a nock-stubbed YouTube + `GOLAZO_OAUTH_MOCK=1` shim, and will work against real YouTube once the operator has run `golazo auth <kid>` for real credentials.
</objective>
